Sam Shea

Digital Content Manager at Printing Industries of America

Sam Shea is the Digital Content Manager at Printing Industries of America. He oversees the day-to-day operations and maintenance of the Association's primary website--www.printing.org--as well as its various microsites. Sam also plays a significant role in the administration and management of the Association's eLearning platform, the iLearning Center, and in the scheduling and execution of educational webinars. In addition to his duties with online content, Sam serves as the primary point of contact for Printing Industries of America's physical publications and as an assistant editor for the Association's magazine.
